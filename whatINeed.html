What do I need?




To join and play with the MHK Chess club you simply need to come to one of our events. Events are open to all ages and playing strengths.
Our club plays a lot of US Chess Federation (USCF) rated games. In order to participate in these events, you will need to join USCF.


Some events will supply equipment, however it is best to bring your own. We suggest buying the following clock and set.
